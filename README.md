This course explores the intersection of computer science, innovation, and entrepreneurship. Key themes include tech-driven market trends, product pitch positioning, cross-team marketing strategy, and the essential soft skills required to drive innovative techno-ventures.

## 💡 Course Reflection & Key Takeaways
This course completely transformed my understanding of how technical products survive outside the laboratory. Working within the marketing and content creation team showed me that even the most brilliant technology needs clear communication, distinct branding, and a precise audience hook to truly make a business impact. Navigating video production and t-shirt design iterations taught me how to align creative output with professional corporate guidelines under tight timelines. Ultimately, hearing from industry trailblazers and interacting with companies like Huawei and RunCloud made me realize that technical skills are only half the equation; the real power lies in matching emerging engineering with strong business empathy and adaptive soft skills to solve human problems at scale.
